Importance of Discipline

Discipline provides people with rules to live their lives efficiently and effectively. When you have discipline in your life you can make sinall sacrifices in the present for a better life in the future. Discipline creates habits, habits make routines, and routines become who you are daily.

Below is a great poem to help drive home the importance of mutines and discipline.

  1. I am your constant companion.
  2. I am your greatest helper or heaviest burden.
  3. I will push you onward or drag you down to failure.
    • Those who are great, I have made great.
    • Those who are failures, I have made failures.
    • Be easy with me and I will destroy you.
  4. I am easily managed - you must be firm with me.
